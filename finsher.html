What does a Finsher do?

The finisher focuses on refining the car’s appearance and protecting its surfaces. This often involves polishing, where the finisher carefully buffs the car’s paint to remove imperfections, scratches, or oxidation, restoring its original shine. The process not only enhances the car’s aesthetic appeal but also helps to protect the paintwork from future wear and environmental damage. Another form of a finsher is ceramic wrapping to the car's exterior. This protective coating shields the paint from UV rays, dirt, water, and minor abrasions, ensuring the car maintains its pristine look and is easier to clean. Although is much more expensive than the other options. This phase is completly optional but recommend as it can help in the long run.
